How it Works

CLV3D is a pool for CLV liquidity provider to earn an additional layer of rewards for providing liquidity.

When you deposit CLV-ETH LP tokens to the CLV3D contract, C3D tokens are minted and added to your balance.

The value of C3D tokens fluctuates based on the amount of CLV-ETH in the CLV3D contract.

There is a 4% entrance and exit fee when you swap CLV-ETH LP tokens for C3D tokens.

100% of the entrance/exit fees are paid as dividends to C3D holders.

This pool is designed to operate like an interest bearing account where participants can freely join and leave and long term C3D holders will be rewarded from the activity of others.
